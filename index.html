🏛️ COLOSSEUM FACTS 🏛️

📍 Location: Rome, Italy

📅 Built: 72-80 AD (8 years)

👑 Emperors: Vespasian (started), Titus (completed), Domitian (modifications)

📏 Dimensions: 189m long, 156m wide, 48m tall (620ft x 513ft x 157ft)

⚖️ Material: Travertine limestone, tuff, brick, concrete

👥 Capacity: 50,000 - 80,000 spectators

🎭 Events: Gladiator fights, animal hunts, executions, naval battles

🔄 Hypogeum: Underground complex with elevators and trapdoors

🏗️ Architecture: 80 entrances, 3 tiers of arches (Doric, Ionic, Corinthian)

⛪ Modern use: Tourist attraction, Catholic ceremonies (Good Friday)

🌍 UNESCO: World Heritage Site since 1980

✨ New 7 Wonders: Named one of the New 7 Wonders of the World in 2007

🎨 Original color: Painted with bright colors (red, blue, green, gold)
